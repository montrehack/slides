# Track instructions ----- You need to find 11 flags (not in that particular order but some might depend on others...) : - The Card OS version. - The Card Serial Number (CSN). ** NOT the CSN in the CPLC / KEYDATA / Card Manager etc.**. - The Protected Derived Key (PDK). - The First Migration Export Key Part (KP1). - The Second Migration Export Key Part (KP2). - The Mother Card Key (MCK). - The Derived Card Key (DCK). - The Original Key (OK). - The Master Key (MK). - The Expense Count Reset Phone Number. - The Visual Flag Printed On The Card. A couple of advices : - Some flags are easy to find, some take commitment. Don't give up. - Hex streams can be PITA, take your time to double-check what you read. I honestly hope you have at least as much fun (and suffering) as I did, designing and implementing the whole track.
